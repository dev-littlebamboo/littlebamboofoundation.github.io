Little Bamboo Foundation

Team

It is essential to provide our children with the best care possible. With this in mind, we have established a team of 15 highly dedicated and qualified professionals in Cebu who work tirelessly to ensure that our little ones receive the care they deserve. Moreover, a committed board convenes quarterly in Cebu to offer support and guidance to the team. Our organization is primarily funded by volunteers based in Europe, where we maintain a small but active team.
