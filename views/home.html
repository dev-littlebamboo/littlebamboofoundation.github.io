Little Bamboo Foundation

Development through education in the Philippines

We are a Filipino charity foundation with a solid track record and roughly 600 beneficiaries with children from the slums of Cebu’s harbor. Our qualified team of 15 Filipinos & an 8-member board runs a preschool, a scholarship, and a family development program.

Our Programs

Education

Following a curriculum centered on the needs of a child, that was created by an expert educational consultant.

Scholarship

Helping children in the public education system after they have left the Little Bamboo pre-school.

Family Development

Reinforcing the link between parents and children and fostering responsible parenthood.

Trusted by numerous Donors Supporters Philanthropists

Cope Family, London

Little Bamboo is one of those rare organisations that truly changes children’s lives. Not just in tiny degrees, but dramatically - giving them hope and prospects where previously they had none. What the team has achieved, despite limited resources, has been stunning. It has been a great pleasure for us to be associated with this project. Many thanks to you all!

Our Philosophy

The parents of Little Bamboo children can give them a good education irrespective of their poverty. By taking part in our program, families commit to either making a small financial contribution, or spending time supporting the school.
