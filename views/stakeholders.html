Stakeholders

Beneficiaries

Because working with young children can make the crucial difference, we start with toddlers and support around 600 children until they have completed their A-Level exams.

Team

Our children deserve the best: a team of 15 qualified and dedicated professionals in Cebu take care of them. In Cebu, a committed Board meets every quarter to challenge and support the team. In Europe, where most of the funds come from, we are a small team of active volunteers.

Partners

For the initial five years, the French supermarket chain Casino provided crucial support to our project. Alongside, Enfants du Mekong, a prominent French charity, has been instrumental in both running and financing our scholarship program.
